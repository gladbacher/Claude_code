# Inequality Content Series

UK wealth inequality content: one long-form newsletter and a five-week short-form video series. Core argument: the ultra-wealthy are not just locking out the young (stage one) — they are extracting assets the middle class already holds (stage two). Intellectual foundation: Gary Stevenson (asset price inflation from concentrated wealth, non-self-correcting), Gabriel Zucman (coordinated minimum wealth tax, ownership transparency).

## Series mechanics

- **Escalating temperature:** conversational → forensic → cold anger → peak → quiet verdict.
- **Escalating CTAs:** follow → share → comment MP's name → write to MP (specific question) → recruit specific people.
- Week 3's CTA (MP name) deliberately seeds week 4's CTA (write to that MP).

## Production

Direct to camera, single light, no stock footage, no gloss. Number cards: plain white on black. Location B-roll (week 4 only): care home exteriors, estate agent windows — no faces, nothing staged. Each script carries its own production notes, including a legal note on week 4 (don't name specific operators without checked sources).

## Tone rules

- Anger controlled and evidenced, never performative.
- Credibility from specificity: named mechanisms, named economists, real numbers.
- Speaks to the middle class as people with skin in the game.
- Never softened for palatability.
