# Script 2 — "The Pension Trap"

**Series position:** Week 2 — first mechanism deep-dive. Temperature: forensic, building.
**Length:** ~85 seconds. Direct to camera. Two number cards.
**CTA level:** Share.

---

## Script

**[HOOK — 0:00–0:07]**
Your pension isn't a savings account. It's a toll road. And you're paying the toll every single year, whether the road goes anywhere or not.

**[0:07–0:25]**
Your parents probably had a defined benefit pension — a promise. Work forty years, get a guaranteed income for life, employer carries the risk. That's gone. You have defined contribution: a pot, invested in markets, and if markets fail, that's your problem now. The risk got transferred from companies to you, and it was called modernisation.

**[0:25–0:50]**
But the risk transfer isn't even the worst part. The worst part is the fees. One and a half percent a year sounds like nothing. It's not nothing.

*[Number card: "1.5% annual fees over 40 years ≈ one third of your pension. Gone."]*

A third of your pot. Not lost — *moved*. Every year, from your retirement to the institution managing it. They get paid whether your fund grows or not. You're working forty years; they're collecting for forty years.

**[0:50–1:10]**
And what's waiting at the end? If you need care — and many of us will — the system is designed to drain what's left, plus your house, before the state steps in. Then whatever survives gets to your kids, minus inheritance tax on thresholds that have been frozen while everything inflated.

*[Number card: "Your pension → fees. Your house → care costs. Your estate → frozen thresholds."]*

Saved. Skimmed. Drained. Intercepted. At every stage, someone is positioned to take a cut. None of them is you.

**[CTA — 1:10–1:20]**
Somebody in your life is paying these fees right now and doesn't know it. Share this with them. That's the whole ask this week.
