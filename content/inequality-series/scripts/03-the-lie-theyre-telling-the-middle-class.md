# Script 3 — "The Lie They're Telling the Middle Class"

**Series position:** Week 3 — naming the misdirection. Temperature: cold anger, controlled.
**Length:** ~90 seconds. Direct to camera. One title card per named lie.
**CTA level:** Comment with your MP's name.

---

## Script

**[HOOK — 0:00–0:08]**
There are three stories being told to keep you from looking at where the money actually went. You've heard all three this week. Let me name them.

**[LIE ONE — 0:08–0:30]**
*[Title card: "1. Intergenerational unfairness"]*
Boomers versus millennials. Homeowners versus renters. Your parents versus your kids. Notice what this frame does: it points your anger sideways and down. Never up. The 55-year-old whose pension is being skimmed and the 25-year-old who can't buy a flat are not enemies — they're at different stages of the same extraction. The frame exists so they never compare notes.

**[LIE TWO — 0:30–0:50]**
*[Title card: "2. Capital flight"]*
"Tax the wealthy and they'll leave." This isn't an argument, it's a veto — it's designed to end the conversation before it starts. Gabriel Zucman has spent his career on exactly this: ownership registries work, coordinated minimum taxes work — we already built one for corporations. The obstacle isn't technical. It's that nobody's been made to do it.

**[LIE THREE — 0:50–1:10]**
*[Title card: "3. There's no money"]*
"Fiscal responsibility." No money for care, no money for housing — in a country sitting on trillions in private wealth, more concentrated every year. The money exists. "There's no money" is only true if you've decided in advance that *that* money doesn't count.

**[CLOSE + CTA — 1:10–1:30]**
Three stories. One job: keep your eyes off the balance sheet at the top. So here's this week's ask, and it's specific. Comment with the name of your MP. Just the name. Next week I'm going to show you the most concrete example of all of this — and you're going to want to know who to ask about it.

## Production notes

- Title cards: white text, black background, numbered. They should feel like charges being read.
- Pace lifts slightly across the three lies; the close comes back down to flat and quiet.
- The MP comment mechanic seeds week 4's CTA — make sure the tease lands.
