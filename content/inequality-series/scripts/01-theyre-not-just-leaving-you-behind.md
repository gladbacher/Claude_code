# Script 1 — "They're Not Just Leaving You Behind"

**Series position:** Week 1 — the reveal. Temperature: controlled, conversational, unsettling.
**Length:** ~75 seconds. Direct to camera, single light, no cutaways except number cards.
**CTA level:** Follow.

---

## Script

**[HOOK — 0:00–0:08]**
If you own a house and have a pension, you've been watching the inequality story thinking it's about young people. It's not. It's about you. You're just in a later chapter.

**[0:08–0:25]**
Here's the mechanism. The ultra-wealthy can't spend their money — there's too much of it. So they invest it. In houses, in companies, in anything that pays. That's why your kids can't buy a house: the prices were bid up by money that has nowhere else to go.

*[Number card: "House prices: 7x average salary. Your parents paid 3x."]*

**[0:25–0:45]**
But locking the young out was stage one. Stage two is the assets you already hold. Your pension pot — paying fees that will eat a third of it over your working life. Your house — earmarked for care costs before your kids ever see it. Your savings — eroded for a decade by zero interest rates, which was policy, not weather.

**[0:45–1:00]**
The money that's coming out of your pension, your house, your savings — it doesn't disappear. It goes somewhere. And the people it goes to would very much like you to keep believing this story is about someone else.

**[CTA — 1:00–1:10]**
Over the next few weeks I'm going to show you each mechanism, one at a time, with numbers. Follow so you don't miss it. Because this only works while you're not looking.

---

## Production notes

- One number card only (7x/3x). Keep everything else on the face.
- No music until the final five seconds, if at all.
- Delivery: like telling a friend something you wish you didn't know. No performance anger — that's later in the series.
