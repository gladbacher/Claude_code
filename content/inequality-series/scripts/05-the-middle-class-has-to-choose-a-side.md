# Script 5 — "The Middle Class Has to Choose a Side"

**Series position:** Week 5 — the call to action. Temperature: resolved, not shouting. The quietest delivery of the series.
**Length:** ~85 seconds. Direct to camera only. No cutaways, one number card.
**CTA level:** Share broadly — recruit, don't just inform.

---

## Script

**[HOOK — 0:00–0:10]**
Four weeks. The pension fees, the care home machine, the three lies. So here's the last question of this series, and it's for the comfortable people: are you sure you're on the right side of the line?

**[THE LINE — 0:10–0:35]**
Because there is a line, and it isn't where you've been told. It's not homeowners versus renters, or old versus young. It's the people whose wealth works for them — who get richer automatically, every year, whatever they do — and everyone else. Everyone whose pension is skimmed, whose savings were eroded, whose house is earmarked for care costs. If your money comes from working, you're on this side of the line. Whatever your house is worth.

**[THE FIX — 0:35–0:55]**
And the fix is not radical. A wealth tax on assets above ten million pounds — that's less than one percent of the population. If you just flinched about your house: it is not about you, and anyone telling you it is, is running the capital flight script on you. Coordinated internationally. Full ownership transparency. No trust loopholes, no offshore carve-outs. Revenue into housing, care, education — the things being stripped.

*[Number card: "Assets over £10m. Fewer than 1 in 100 people. Probably not you."]*

**[THE WINDOW — 0:55–1:15]**
Here's why it's urgent. The middle class still gets listened to because it still owns things. Every year of extraction takes a little more of that leverage away. A middle class with no assets is just a bigger working class — with no seat at the table. This gets fixed while you still have power, or it doesn't get fixed.

**[CTA — 1:15–1:30]**
So: share this series. Not to your followers — to specific people. The friend with the pension pot. Your parents. The one who says politics isn't for them. Five videos, the whole argument. The only thing this needs to work is more people who've seen the machine. Be the reason one more person sees it.

---

## Production notes

- Lowest energy delivery of the series. The escalation has happened; this is the verdict.
- "Are you sure you're on the right side of the line?" — hold a beat of silence after it. That's the hook for the whole series in retrospect cuts.
- End on the face, not a card. No outro graphic.
